Our Mission

Walking Alongside Women into Freedom

Inn From the Storm was founded with a simple but powerful belief: no woman should navigate life after incarceration alone. We serve as a bridge between the system and a better future — providing the tools, connections, and community that make lasting change possible.

Our focus on African American women reflects the disproportionate impact the justice system has on our community. Through education, legal support, health resources, and wraparound services, we are rewriting the narrative — one woman at a time.

The Data Speaks

Why This Work Matters

Research from the CDC and State of Missouri reveals the urgent need for reentry support — especially for African American women.

2x
African American women are incarcerated at twice the rate of white women nationally
60%
Of women returning face housing instability within their first year of release
5x
Incarcerated women face higher HIV/AIDS rates than the general population
44%
National recidivism rate for women returning to prison within 5 years

Our participants? We've only lost 2 to the system.

While national recidivism rates remain stubbornly high, Inn From the Storm's comprehensive approach — combining legal support, education, health services, and genuine community — is changing outcomes. Our Hand in Hand mentorship model proves that with the right support, women don't go back.

Data sourced from CDC reports, Missouri Department of Corrections, and Bureau of Justice Statistics

What We Offer

All Services Are Completely Free

From the moment you come home, we're here. Every service we provide is free of charge — because rebuilding shouldn't come with a price tag.

Legal Assistance

Connection to attorneys, court navigation support, and guidance through every stage of the legal process. We help you understand your rights and options.

Attorney Referrals

Education & GED

Access to GED programs, vocational training, and educational resources that open doors to new careers and possibilities.

GED Programs

Health & Wellness

Comprehensive health support including HIV/AIDS resources, mental health services, substance abuse counseling, and connection to healthcare providers.

HIV/AIDS Support

Housing Support

Assistance finding safe, stable housing — because having a place to call home is the foundation for everything else.

Transitional Housing

Employment Help

Resume building, job placement assistance, interview preparation, and partnerships with employers who believe in second chances.

Job Placement

Hand in Hand Mentorship

Our signature peer mentorship program connects women who've successfully reintegrated with those just beginning their journey home.

Peer Support

Accepting self-referrals, attorney referrals, and court referrals
